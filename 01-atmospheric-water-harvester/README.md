## The Problem

2.2 billion people lack safely managed drinking water. Atmospheric water generators exist but use compressors (energy-intensive) or desiccants (slow, need regeneration). They work best in humid climates but fail in arid regions where water is needed most.

## The Solution

Superconducting coils generate precise magnetic fields that manipulate the dew point of air passing through the device. The magnetic field lowers the activation energy for water molecule clustering, causing condensation at higher temperatures than conventional methods.

**How it works:**
1. Air drawn through superconducting coil array
2. Magnetic field causes water molecules to cluster
3. Condensation occurs at 25-30°C (vs 15°C conventional)
4. Water collected in reservoir
5. No compressor, no refrigerant, no moving parts
6. Runs on 12V solar panel

**Components:**
- RTS coil array (field shaping)
- Condensation surface (hydrophobic coating)
- Collection reservoir
- Solar panel + battery
- Filtration system (standard)

**Novel contribution:** Magnetic field manipulation of dew point is a new physical effect. Existing AWGs need 500W+ compressors. This needs 50W.
