## The Problem

Water quality testing requires laboratory analysis ($50-500 per sample, 2-7 day turnaround). In developing countries, testing is rare — people drink water they can't verify. 2 billion people use contaminated water without knowing it.

## The Solution

Superconducting sensors detect contaminants through their magnetic signatures. Different molecules have different magnetic susceptibilities and produce distinct spectra. The sensor identifies contaminants in real-time.

**How it works:**
1. Water sample enters sensor chamber
2. Superconducting coil applies magnetic field
3. Contaminant molecules produce characteristic spectra
4. AI identifies 100+ contaminants
5. Results displayed on screen in seconds
6. Continuous monitoring mode available

**Components:**
- RTS SQUID sensor
- Sample introduction system
- AI processing unit (laptop-scale)
- Display interface
- Solar/battery power

**Novel contribution:** RTS SQUID sensors provide the sensitivity needed for parts-per-trillion detection in the field. Lab-grade analysis in a handheld device.
